## Rollback Procedure

**When to rollback:** If critical P0 feature is broken and affecting >50% of users

| **Step** | **Action**                           | **Owner**  | **Time** |
| -------- | ------------------------------------ | ---------- | -------- |
| 1        | Identify issue severity (P0/P1/P2)   | [Product]  | 5 min    |
| 2        | Decision: Fix forward or rollback?   | [Lead Dev] | 5 min    |
| 3        | If rollback: Deploy previous version | [Dev]      | 10 min   |
| 4        | Verify rollback success              | [QA]       | 10 min   |
| 5        | Post incident notice (status page)   | [Product]  | 5 min    |
| 6        | Email affected users (if any)        | [Support]  | 15 min   |

**Total rollback time: ~50 minutes**

### Bug Severity Definitions

**P0 (Critical):**

- App completely down
- Cannot sign up or log in
- Payment processing broken
- Data loss

**Action:** Immediate rollback or hotfix within 1 hour

**P1 (High):**

- Core feature broken (tasks, AI chat)
- Affects >25% of users
- Workaround exists but difficult

**Action:** Fix within 24 hours

**P2 (Medium):**

- Minor feature broken
- Affects <25% of users
- Easy workaround exists

**Action:** Fix in next sprint
